📉 Analytics & Pattern Intelligence
Deep patterns extracted from 298,450 actual BTP violation records
01
Night Dominance: 75.4% of violations (225,169) occur between 10PM–6AM. Enforcement must be restructured for night shifts.
02
5AM Peak: 34,085 violations at 5AM alone — 11.4% of all records. This is the single highest-risk hour for illegal parking.
03
Holiday Surge: January 2024 peaked at 65,813 violations — 32% above monthly average. Festival & holiday periods need pre-emptive deployment.
04
Sunday Risk: Sundays average 46,863 violations vs Monday's 38,931 — a 20.4% higher weekend risk. Weekend patrol intensity should be higher.
05
Enforcement Lag: Avg 468h (19.5 days) from violation to SCITA submission. Real-time systems could reduce this to <5 minutes.
06
28.8% Rejection Rate: Nearly 1 in 3 validated records rejected — indicating data quality problems at the capture point (officer training gap).
07
Bus Severity: Buses average 1.78 violations per case vs scooters' 1.11. One illegally parked bus = 4-lane equivalent blockage.
08
Repeat Offenders: 35,587 vehicles (15.4%) have 2+ violations. Top offender: 55 violations. Watch-list + escalated challans needed.
